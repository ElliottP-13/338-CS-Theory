\section*{Problem 2}

\noindent
Decide whether the following grammar is ambiguous.
\newline

$S\rightarrow AB|aaB$

$A\rightarrow a|Aa$

$B\rightarrow b$


\begin{table}[h!]
\begin{tabular}{l r}
S & S\\
AB & aaB\\
AaB & aaB\\
aaB & aaB\\
aab & aab\\
\end{tabular}

\end{table}


There are multiple ways to generate the string ``aab'' with this grammar, so \textbf{yes} the grammar is ambiguous.
